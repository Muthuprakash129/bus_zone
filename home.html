ABOUT MKCE

The Best Engineering College With 23 Years of Excellence.


M.Kumarasamy College of Engineering is an Autonomous Institution, Approved by AICTE, New Delhi and Affiliated to Anna University, Chennai it was established in the year 2000 by M.Kumarasamy Health and Educational Trust with a vision to provide technical education for all the sectors of the society. The institution with its state of the art facilities and dedicated team of faculty members has become a hub of technocrats and is marching towards new frontiers.
